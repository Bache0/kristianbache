FOLKETS SKOLE

Web design

In Denmark the public school is fighting for it's survival. More and more parents decide to put their children in private school.

They do it because they feel their child is going unoticed and not getting enough attention by the teachers.

  • Stories

  • Responsive design
  • posters

Back

This concept adresses the problem by praising public school "warriors". Parents or adults contribute on the page with people from their time in public school. People who made a difference for their education and took part in forming who they are today.

Landing Page

The landing page features four different stories about four different warriors. You can either click on one story and read more, or explore the other warriors.

Explore the warriors

If you want to see all warriors, you can explore a mosaic of all the different stories. You can drag around to find interesting people, or filter between friends, parents or teachers.

Stories

Read the personal experiences, stories and thoughts of the person writing about his/her warrior.

Praise warriors

Everywhere on the site you have the opportunity to praise your own warrior. Select the button in the menu and tell your story.
