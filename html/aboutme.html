ABOUT ME

Hello!


I'm a Dane from Norway with a love for graphic design. I'm currently working as a graphic designer in the creative world.

My passion lies in cooking, reading, playing keyboard/guitar and reading. I also love to travel around and experience different cultures, especially in Europe. I moved to Denmark when i was 18 years old, and there i started my pursuit for a career in graphic design.

I've always been interested in creating and designing since i was a child. I loved lego and the endless possibilites to create. I was encouraged to be creative, and i learned a lot of crafts and their possibilites. After some time i started to use Photoshop, and from there i made projects for local clients and soon after decided my career/education choice as a graphic designer.
